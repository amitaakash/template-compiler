1. PREFACE

IRCON International Limited, on behalf of Chhattisgarh East Railway Limited, is executing the construction of a new B.G. Electrified Single Railway Line for the Chhattisgarh East Rail Corridor Phase-II. The project spans from Urga (Km 0) to Dharamjaigarh (Km 62) via Bhaisma station, Dongaama, Dhinara, Dhaskamunda, Hati, and Khadgaon in the state of Chhattisgarh over the South East Central Railway. This specific package (Package-1A) involves civil works between Urga (Km 0) to Ex. Dhaskamunda (Km 30), including earthwork for railway formation, blanketing work, minor bridges, RUBs, station buildings, service buildings, staff quarters, and platform works including general electrical works. The section also includes a double track between CH-1.4 Km to CH-2.3 Km. The project is being executed on a National Competitive Bidding basis with a stipulated completion period of 18 months from the date of issue of the Letter of Acceptance.

IRCON invited online Open tenders under Two Packet (Vide e-Procurement Notice No.: IRCON/2059/CGRP/e-TENDER/23-24/Urga-Dharamjaigarh/Pkg-1A (R) Dated: 07.02.2024 for the above said work. The bidding documents duly approved by the competent authority were uploaded in CPP website www.etendergov.in/eprocure/app on 07.02.2024).

The approved tender document for subject work is kept at CP-001 - CP-001 Tender File. The estimated cost of tender is Rs 121,93,52,991.81 (Basic Amount) Crs. Approval of CA is placed at NP-001 of Tender File.

Notification for the NIT was published in leading newspaper (CP-001 - CP-001 of tender file ). The e-tender was scheduled to be submitted on 27.02.2024 up to 15:00 Hrs Hrs and opened on 28.02.2024 at 15:00 Hrs Hrs. The tender documents along with all Annexure were uploaded on e-tender web site i.e https.//www.eprocure.gov.in.
